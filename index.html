fast fashion

The rise of fast, disposable fashion has left an enormous environmental footprint that is mostly invisible to the public. Its impact spans the globe: from the countries that cultivate raw materials and manufacture clothing, to the oceans and air used to transport them, to the consumer countries where the clothes are retailed.

This poster series aims to raise public awareness of fast fashion's total environmental impact, and to draw attention to our individual responsibilities as fashion's consumers.
